LOTLOGIC is committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection:
LOTLOGIC does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All extension data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage.

What We Store Locally:
- Saved auction lots (URLs, vehicle details, scrap values)
- Your settings (fees, transport costs, target profit)
- Anonymous usage statistics (lot count, average values)

This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Contains no personal information
- Can be deleted at any time through the extension settings

What We DON'T Collect:
- Names, emails, or contact information
- Payment or financial information
- Browsing history
- Location data
- Any personally identifiable information

Third-Party Services:
LOTLOGIC does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Data Sharing:
We do not share, sell, or transmit any user data to third parties.
